## 🎯 Learning Outcomes

By the end of this course, you should be able to:

- ✅ Describe the **Workato ONE Platform** and its three core layers (Agentic, Orchestrate, Data/Apps/Systems)
- ✅ Understand Workato's **decoupled architecture** and how recipes, connections, triggers, and steps fit together
- ✅ Design recipes using **data mapping**, **formula mode**, **conditional actions**, and **repeat actions**
- ✅ Implement **error monitoring and handling** in recipes
- ✅ Use the **Operations Hub Dashboard** to monitor recipe health and troubleshoot failures
- ✅ Manage **recipe versions** and apply version-control best practices
- ✅ Interpret **job reports** and understand task usage
