## 🏛️ **The three pillars of Workflow Apps**

Creating Workflow Apps is simple — Workato streamlines it by providing a **unified interface** where you generate all the necessary assets (data tables, recipes, pages) for your app from one place.

> 📌 Every Workflow App is built on three pillars:
> 
> 1. **🖥️ User Interface**
> 2. **💾 Data Storage**
> 3. **🍳 Business Logic**

(These are the same three elements introduced in 10.1 — here we look at each in detail.)

---

### 🖥️ User Interface

The UI represents what users see and how they interact with the app.

> 📌 Workflow Apps comes with a **drag-and-drop UI designer** that enables creation of aesthetically pleasing, **fully customizable interfaces** — no coding required.

---

### 💾 Data Storage

> 📌 **Data tables** provide a stable location for storing workflow data. Tables are configured directly in the Workato UI without needing data engineering expertise.

Standard **CRUD operations** are supported: **Create, Read, Update, Delete**.

---

### 🍳 Business Logic

Business logic is implemented through **recipes** built using the familiar Workato recipe builder.

> 📌 The **Workflow Apps connector** lets you combine **automated business logic with manual human review steps** — the key to robust workflows that need approvals or human oversight.

## 🚪 **Apps Portal**

> 📌 The **Apps Portal** is the central hub for **configuring settings, managing users, and organizing groups** for your Workflow Apps.

Navigation path:

> **Workato homepage → Tools → Apps Portal**

---

### 👥 Adding users and groups

Under the Apps Portal, you can configure access by creating users and groups — ensuring each user only sees workflows relevant to them.

---

### ⚙️ Settings

Access via: **Tools → Apps Portal → Settings**. There are **three segments**:

|Segment|Purpose|
|---|---|
|**🔧 General**|Tailor the **name and URL** for accessing your Apps Portal.|
|**🎨 Branding**|Personalize the portal in alignment with your brand identity.|
|**🔐 Security**|Control **authentication methods** and settings for **iframe embedding**.|

---

### 🔐 Security — authentication methods

Workato supports **two authentication methods** for Apps Portals:

|Method|Notes|
|---|---|
|**🔑 Password-based authentication**|Standard username/password login.|
|**🪪 SAML-based Single Sign-On (SSO)**|Users log in with corporate credentials. Comes with additional options (below).|

> 📌 **SAML SSO additional options:**
> 
> - **🤖 JIT (Just-In-Time) provisioning** — automatically create users in the system on first login.
> - **🏷️ Custom SAML attributes** — automatically assign users to groups.
> - **🌐 Per-domain SSO enforcement** — enforce SSO only for specific domains; users outside the org can still log in with username/password.

More info in the [Workflow Apps SAML documentation](https://docs.workato.com/workflow-apps/saml.html).

---

### 🖼️ Security — iframe embedding

To enable iframe embedding, toggle the **"Enable iframe embedding"** option. You can either allow embedding across all iframes, or restrict it to designated domains.

> 📌 To restrict embedding to specific domains, enter URLs in the **"Specify frame-ancestors"** field. These URLs populate the **Content-Security-Policy HTTP header** that controls iframe rendering of your Workflow Apps.

If you leave **Specify frame-ancestors empty**, pages can be embedded in any iframe without constraint.

## 🚀 **Module key takeaways**

- **Three pillars**: UI (drag-and-drop designer), Data Storage (data tables with full CRUD), Business Logic (recipes — plus the Workflow Apps connector for human-review steps).
- **Apps Portal** is the central control plane: `Tools → Apps Portal`. Configures users, groups, and three settings segments — **General, Branding, Security**.
- **Two auth methods**: Password-based + SAML SSO. SSO comes with three layered options: **JIT provisioning, custom SAML attributes, per-domain enforcement**.
- **Iframe embedding** is controlled via the **Content-Security-Policy `frame-ancestors`** directive — toggle ON globally, restrict to specific domains, or leave open by clearing the field.
