## 🧱 **What is Workflow Apps?**

> 📌 **Workato Workflow Apps** is a **low-code/no-code application platform** for **rapidly building workflow applications on Workato**.

The platform enables IT and business teams to quickly build apps without code — streamlining workflows, reducing friction, and freeing teams to focus on their actual goals instead of the plumbing.

---

## 🎯 **Common use cases**

Workflow Apps can power a wide range of business workflows:

- **✅ Approval / request processes**
- **🚪 Onboarding / offboarding procedures**
- **⚡ Trigger automated processes**
- **🖱️ User-friendly UIs**
- **👀 Customer review processes**
- **📋 Purchase Order request forms**
- **🆕 Feature request & NDA submissions**
- **📌 Task management**
- **📄 Document processing**
- **⚠️ Exception management**

And many more — the common thread is **well-defined processes involving multiple people and systems** that benefit from a unified app surface.

---

## ⚙️ **Three fundamental elements**

Every Workflow App is built from three components. These are covered in detail in lesson 10.2:

- **🖥️ User Interface** — what users see and interact with.
- **💾 Data Layer** — where workflow data is stored.
- **🍳 Business Logic** — the recipes that power workflow execution.

> 📌 Workflow Apps lets you **integrate complex business logic and rules** into your processes — and you build the responsive UI through a **drag-and-drop interface, wizard, and templates** rather than writing code.

---

## ✨ **Advantages of Workflow Apps**

Five distinct advantages, each tied to a specific design choice in the platform:

- **🔄 Transform workflows into interactive applications** — simplify the complexity of building process-centric apps so everyone in your organization can accelerate business outcomes and reduce costs.
- **🎨 Easily build apps without code** — empower IT and business teams to build responsive workflow apps with **drag-and-drop ease**.
- **📋 Manage every stage of your workflow** — quickly create, organize, and implement each stage.
- **🧮 Visually incorporate business logic** — combine **automated business logic with manual human validation** to build robust workflows.
- **🌐 Unify your business applications** — access all your Workflow apps **under a single domain**, with proactive access management through users and groups, including **SSO Just-in-Time provisioning**.

> 📌 Workato provides **1,200+ prebuilt connectors** — the library that powers the "visually incorporate business logic" advantage and what makes drag-and-drop business logic feasible at enterprise scope.

> 📌 **Single domain + SSO JIT provisioning** means all your Workflow Apps live under one unified URL and access surface — users get accounts created automatically on first SSO login.

- **Workflow Apps = low-code/no-code platform** for building business workflow applications on Workato.
- **Three fundamental elements**: User Interface (drag-and-drop), Data Layer (storage), Business Logic (recipes).
