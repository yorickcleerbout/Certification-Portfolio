## ⚙️ **Actions for recipe & job management**

RecipeOps by Workato also works in **action steps**. Three actions let you actively manage recipes and jobs in your account:

|Action|What it does|Notes|
|---|---|---|
|**▶️ Start Recipe**|Specify a recipe to be started.|Single recipe — by name OR Recipe ID. **Job fails with error** if the recipe can't be started (e.g. apps not connected).|
|**⏹️ Stop Recipe**|Specify a recipe to be stopped.|Same selection options as Start.|
|**🔁 Rerun Jobs**|Re-run specified recipe jobs.|**Requires a Job ID.** Usually retrieved by an upstream RecipeOps trigger or action.|

> 📌 **Rerun Jobs requires a Job ID** — that's the linking key that connects this action to whichever upstream RecipeOps step (trigger or action) produced the job to be rerun.

> ⚠️ A **Start Recipe** action will fail if any of the apps in the recipe aren't connected. Pre-flight your connections.

---

## 🔍 **Actions for information gathering**

RecipeOps also exposes **read-only actions** that produce output datapills for downstream steps. Six actions for information gathering:

|Action|What it returns|
|---|---|
|**🪪 Get account details**|Information about the Workato account.|
|**🍳 Get recipe details**|Details for a specific recipe.|
|**🔌 List connections**|All connections in the workspace.|
|**📋 List recipes**|All recipes in the workspace.|
|**🔎 Search job history**|Job execution history with filters.|
|**🔍 Search recipes**|Recipes filtered by status or connection ID.|

---

### 🔎 Search Job History — the key details

> 📌 Search Job History retrieves the **last 1,000 jobs by default**. You can filter by **job status** or **creation date range**.

Common pattern: retrieve **failed jobs** to build a recipe that **reruns those failed jobs automatically** using RecipeOps.

---

### 🔍 Search Recipes — vs List Recipes

> 📌 **Search Recipes** lets you **filter by status OR connection ID** — output limited to **1,000 recently updated recipes**.

The key difference from List Recipes:

> 📌 **Search Recipes** can filter by **connection ID**; **List Recipes** cannot. Reach for Search Recipes when you need "all recipes using this specific connection."

## 🚀 **Module key takeaways**

- **Three management actions**: Start Recipe, Stop Recipe (by name or ID), Rerun Jobs (needs Job ID — usually from an upstream trigger/action).
- **Six info-gathering actions** producing output datapills for downstream steps.
- **Search Job History** = last 1,000 jobs by default, filterable by status or creation date — perfect for finding failed jobs to rerun.
- **Search Recipes ≠ List Recipes** — Search adds **filter by connection ID**, which is essential for "find everything using this connection" use cases.
- **Pre-flight your connections** before any Start Recipe action — disconnected apps will fail the start.
