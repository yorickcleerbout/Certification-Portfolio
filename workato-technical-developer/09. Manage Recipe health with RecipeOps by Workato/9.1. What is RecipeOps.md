## 🤖 **What is RecipeOps?**

> 📌 **Recipe operations (RecipeOps)** are the practices around **monitoring and maintaining the status, health, and uptime of automation recipes**. Workato provides RecipeOps as an **all-in-one connector** so you can automate these operational concerns.

The connector covers three areas:

1. **📡 Recipe monitoring**
2. **🚑 Automated recovery**
3. **🧠 Intelligent operations**

You can use RecipeOps to **orchestrate monitoring and recovery** from errors, perform **administrative functions** (retrieving account/recipe/job details), and **automate responses** to stopped recipes and failed jobs — mitigating the impact on critical business processes.

---

## ⚙️ **The three key functions**

### 📡 Recipe monitoring

Use RecipeOps to **increase uptime** of mission-critical automations, reduce operations workload/cost, and **scale faster** with autonomous responses to anomalies.

Specific monitoring capabilities:

- **🗓️ Planned maintenance handling** — monitor automations impacted by planned maintenance, **automatically shut them down** before the window, and turn them back on after. Workato then **automatically catches up on business events** that weren't processed during the downtime.
- **📡 Real-time log streaming** — stream automation job data into **Splunk** or **Sumo Logic** for instant analytics and insights.

---

### 🚑 Automated recovery

Reduce unplanned downtime with **customized workflows based on error types and messages**. These ensure **lossless pause, resume, and auto-retry**.

> 💡 _Example:_ a **custom incident management workflow** built with **ServiceNow, PagerDuty, Twilio**, or others — so Ops teams respond faster.

---

### 🧠 Intelligent operations

Build **custom rule-based bots** for error reporting and recovery:

- **🤖 Error reporting bots** — send error information to external monitoring apps (databases, Splunk) for further analysis. Centralized view across all operations.
- **👤 Real-time user creation notifications** — bot can publish new-user info (user ID, email, name, provisioning method) for real-time visibility into onboarding activity.

---

## 🚀 **Module key takeaways**

- **RecipeOps = automation monitoring + recovery + intelligence** packaged as a single Workato connector.
- Three core functions: **recipe monitoring** (uptime, planned-maintenance handling, log streaming), **automated recovery** (lossless pause/resume/retry, custom incident workflows), and **intelligent operations** (rule-based bots, real-time notifications).
- Workato **auto-catches-up on missed events** when recipes resume after planned downtime — no manual replay needed.
- Stream logs to **Splunk** or **Sumo Logic** for analytics; integrate with **ServiceNow / PagerDuty / Twilio** for incident management.
