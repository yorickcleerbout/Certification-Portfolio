## 📊 **What is Insights?**

> 📌 **Insights** is a **data visualization and manipulation engine** built into Workato's no-code environment, designed to help you **measure the impact of your automation projects**.

With Insights, you can **connect to various data sources** and visualize them through **custom dashboards** — all inside Workato, no external BI tool required.

## 🎯 **Use cases**

Insights addresses several critical use cases around workspace usage, automation impact, and process efficiency. The four canonical use cases:

|Use case|What it measures|
|---|---|
|**📈 Usage Reporting**|Workato platform usage — recipe activity, task consumption, errors.|
|**💰 Automation ROI**|Business impact of automation — dollars/hours saved, error reduction.|
|**⚙️ Process Analytics**|Efficiency and performance of automated business processes via process-specific KPIs.|
|**📱 App Analytics**|User-centric metrics for processes implemented as Workflow Apps.|

---

### 📈 Usage Reporting

Track your Workato **usage and consumption**. See how a workspace is used, including:

- **✅❌ Recipe success rates and errors**
- **⚙️ Recipe and task consumption**
- **📁 Data breakdown by projects and environments**
- **📜 Full job history**

Common visualized metrics: **number of active recipes**, **task consumption over time**, **errors by recipe in a week**. This is about visualizing operational efficiency and Workato platform usage.

---

### 💰 Automation ROI

**Measure the impact of automation on business processes** — financial and time savings, reduced manual errors, increased operational efficiency. Critical for justifying and evaluating automation projects.

Example ROI metrics:

- **💵 Dollars saved**
- **⏱️ Hours saved**
- **🏢 Savings by process or department**

---

### ⚙️ Process Analytics

Focuses on the **performance and efficiency of automated business processes** using **process-specific KPIs**.

Example process metrics:

- **📦 Number of orders processed**
- **⏱️ Average user processing time**
- **🌍 Orders by region**

---

### 📱 App Analytics

Focuses on **user-centric processes implemented as Workflow Apps** — metrics tied to actual human usage of your apps.

Example app metrics:

- **🎫 Number of tickets created**
- **✅ Average time to approve processes**
- **🚪 Average time to complete employee onboarding**

## 🧰 **Insights features (from the knowledge check)**

The knowledge check below tests several Insights product mechanics that aren't spelled out in the lesson body. Worth surfacing here for study:

> 📌 **Real-time data**: Insights displays data **in real time** — not daily updates, weekly summaries, or monthly reports.

> 📌 **Visualization widgets** include **Line chart**, **Bar chart**, and **Pie chart** (among others). _ECG_ is not a widget type — obvious distractor, but useful to lock in.

> 📌 **Starting a dashboard**: the required first step is **selecting a main data source** — before you pick charts, colors, or anything else.

> 📌 **Working with Insights** includes a step to **transform your data** (not upload images, not encrypt, not delete unused data).

> 📌 **Who can view live dashboards**: **both Workato users AND business users via the Workflow Apps portal**. Insights dashboards aren't locked to Workato-native accounts — they can be surfaced through Workflow Apps for non-Workato users.

## 🚀 **Module key takeaways**

- **Insights = built-in visualization + manipulation engine** for measuring automation impact.
- **Four use cases** map cleanly to different audiences and questions:
    - **Usage Reporting** ← IT/ops: "how healthy is the platform?"
    - **Automation ROI** ← finance/leadership: "what's the automation worth?"
    - **Process Analytics** ← business process owners: "how efficient are the processes?"
    - **App Analytics** ← Workflow App owners: "how are users interacting?"
- **Real-time data**, not batched updates.
- **Start dashboards** by selecting the main data source; **transform data** is a working step.
- **Dual audience**: both Workato users and business users (via Workflow Apps portal) can view live dashboards.
