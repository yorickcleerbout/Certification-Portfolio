## ⚙️ **What is BPA?**

> 📌 **Business Process Automation (BPA)** is the use of technology to streamline or transform a specific part of a process — or an entire workflow.

The specific outcomes depend on the use case, but process automation typically delivers four benefits:

- **⏱️ Time savings** — work that used to be manual happens automatically.
- **✅ Fewer errors** — automated execution eliminates the human mistakes that come with repetitive tasks.
- **💰 Lower costs** — less time and fewer errors translate directly into operational savings.
- **🤝 Improved stakeholder experience** — internal teams, customers, and partners interact with a smoother process.

---

### 🛣️ Two approaches to automation

Generally speaking, automation falls into two approaches:

- **🤖 Robotic Process Automation (RPA)**
- **🔗 Integration-led automation**
