## 🤖 **The Future is Workato AI**

AI is here to stay. For most enterprises, though, the real challenge isn't choosing the right model — it's making sure their mission-critical **data, systems, and workflows** are ready for AI adoption.

> 📌 AI doesn't thrive in restrictive silos or brittle integrations. It requires **clean data**, **seamless automation**, and **the ability to operate with context**.

This is where Workato fits in. Workato isn't just an integration platform — it acts as the **operational backbone** that transforms a business into an AI-capable enterprise.

---

### 🎼 Orchestration vs simple data transfer

The knowledge check below tests an important distinction:

> 📌 **Orchestration provides context to AI** by tracking **actions, prompts, and events in real time**. Simple data transfer only moves information — it doesn't enrich the AI with the context needed to act intelligently.

In other words: connecting systems isn't enough on its own. The AI needs to know _what just happened_ and _why_, not just receive raw data.
