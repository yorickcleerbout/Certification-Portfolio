## 🎯 Learning Outcomes

By the end of this course, you should be able to:

- ✅ Define **iPaaS** and explain its five core capabilities (move, map, transform, assess, enforce policies)
- ✅ Distinguish between **data**, **application**, and **cloud integration** and apply the right one to a given scenario
- ✅ Describe Workato's role in a modern data platform — **ingesting** data into warehouses/lakes _and_ **pushing insights back out** to business apps
- ✅ Choose the right Workato connectivity tool for a scenario: **batch processing**, **FileStorage**, **Data Tables**, **OPA smart shunt**, or the **Enterprise Data Hub Accelerator**
- ✅ Recall the hard limits that come up in scenario questions: **10 GB / 100 GB** for FileStorage and **~50,000 records** for SQL Collection
- ✅ Decide between **SQL Collection** (temporary, small/medium datasets) and **SQL Transformations** (large datasets, CDC, persistent)
- ✅ Explain the **five benefits of API Management** and apply security best practices (tokens-as-passwords, periodic refresh, caching, rate limits)
- ✅ Define **Business Process Automation (BPA)** and name its two general approaches
- ✅ Articulate Workato's positioning as the **operational backbone** for an AI-capable enterprise
- ✅ Explain why **orchestration** enriches AI with context that simple data transfer cannot
