## 🎯 **What is the GEARS Framework?**

> 📌 The **GEARS Framework** is a collection of **guides, best practices, and assets** that help you build, maintain, and mature your automation practice.

It's centered around the concept of **Automation HQ** — Workato's guidance on how to structure teams for better alignment with your automation strategy.

> 📌 The framework provides a **systematic approach** for organizations to deliver higher business value through automation by **balancing execution speed and agility with governance and controls**.

That balance — moving fast **while** managing risk — is the central tension GEARS is designed to resolve.

---

## 🔠 **The five domains of GEARS**

**GEARS** is an acronym. Each letter is a domain:

|Letter|Domain|What it focuses on|
|---|---|---|
|**G**|🛡️ **Govern**|How the organization controls, secures, and shapes what automations get built|
|**E**|📚 **Enable**|How the organization equips builders and stakeholders with knowledge and reusable assets|
|**A**|🤝 **Adopt**|How the organization spreads and grows a culture of automation|
|**R**|🏃 **Run**|How the organization operates automations day-to-day|
|**S**|📈 **Scale**|How the organization matures and expands the practice enterprise-wide|

The rest of this chapter goes through each domain in detail (lessons 3.4–3.8), then covers the phased execution approach (3.9).
