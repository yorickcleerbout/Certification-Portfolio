## 👥 **The multi-team reality**

As enterprise automation gathers momentum, it's normal for **multiple teams and users** to share a single Workato workspace. Automation builders might be:

- **💻 IT automation** — help-desk management, data pipelines
- **💰 Finance automation** — month-end reconciliation, invoice processing, procure-to-pay
- **📈 Business processes** — spanning multiple systems and department boundaries

In reality, you'll likely have many more team members across the organization looking to use Workato for their business processes. **Many recipes are built collaboratively** as team members share knowledge and ideas to automate complex business processes together.

> 📌 **Workato Team capabilities enable agile, iterative development through collaboration** — while giving admins the **governance and security tools** needed to ensure compliance.

---

## 🛡️ **Need for governance**

> 📌 **Governance is crucial** for any platform that lets business users work with corporate data — and Workato is no different.

Workato automates diverse business processes by **connecting to different business applications** and processing data across **development, test, and production environments**. Two things must be guaranteed:

- **🔒 Business systems are secured** against unintended activities by platform users.
- **🕵️ Sensitive data isn't accessible** to unauthorized parties.

> 💡 _Example: HR onboarding automation._ You need proper security controls so different HR team members have different levels of responsibility and visibility. Enforcing **access controls** ensures employee personal information is accessible **only to authorized HR members** — not the broader team.

---

## ✅ **Six benefits of governance**

A well-governed Workato workspace delivers concretely on six fronts:

- **🎯 Improve consistency of execution** — a well-defined process to manage automation projects across the organization.
- **🚀 Frictionless onboarding** — new team members get up and running with the appropriate privileges, boosting productivity.
- **🏷️ Promote accountability** — unambiguous asset ownership and good governance practices between teams in a shared workspace.
- **📜 Ensure compliance** — with the organization's security policies and any industry-specific regulatory guidelines.
- **⚡ Minimize administrative overheads** — streamlined workspace management and audit procedures.
- **🤝 Attain leadership trust** — secure and compliant platform usage enables broad personas across the organization (with different technical skills) to build automation.

> 📌 Notice how each benefit maps to a different stakeholder: consistency → the org; onboarding/productivity → new builders; accountability → team owners; compliance → security/legal; overhead reduction → admins; trust → leadership.
