## 📝 **Activity audit log**

> ⚠️ The **Activity audit log is an add-on feature.** Contact your Customer Success Manager for more info.

As team size and project complexity grow, **control and activity visibility** in your Workato workspace become critical. Activity audit logs let you **view changes made by users in your account over time**.

---

### 🔎 What is the Activity audit log?

> 📌 **Activity audit logs** provide a **powerful interface for Workato administrators to track changes comprehensively** in their accounts. Changes include:
> 
> - **👤 User activity** — logins, invitations
> - **🍳 Recipe modifications and events**
> - And more.

---

### 💡 How to use Activity audit logs

Using audit logs, you can **quickly identify changes made in your account and analyze their impact**.

> 💡 _Example:_ An unplanned stoppage of a business-critical **employee onboarding recipe**. Activity audit logs can pinpoint **when the recipe was stopped and which user stopped it** — enabling rapid diagnosis.

---

### 🎫 How to get the Activity audit log feature

Activity audit log is an **add-on**. Contact your **Workato Customer Success Manager** for details.

---

### ⏱️ Log retention

> 📌 **Default retention: 1 year from the event.** Contact your Customer Success Manager for a longer retention period.

Alternatively, use the **Audit log streaming** feature to replicate log data to a destination of your choice.

### 👥 Who can view Activity audit logs?

Once enabled, **three types of users** can view Activity audit logs:

- **👑 The account owner**
- **🛡️ Users with the Admin role**
- **🔐 Users with the Activity audit privilege**

> 📌 Three viewer groups — remember them: account owner, Admin role users, Activity audit privilege users.

## 🙈 **Data masking**

Workato job history stores **data going in and out of every recipe step**. For sensitive data — employee **SSN, date of birth**, etc. — this shouldn't be visible to Workato account admins.

> 📌 **Data masking** lets users **disable data display at the trigger and step level** — control at every step of the recipe.

> ⚠️ Data masking is **available only for specific users** — contact your Customer Success Manager to learn more.

---

### ✅ Enabling data masking

Data can be masked **for individual recipe steps** — input and output data displays will be disabled in job history.

> 📌 To turn off display for a specific step: **check the "Enable data masking" field, found under the Comment section for each recipe step**.

Per-step control is granular by design — you don't have to mask an entire recipe. Just the sensitive steps.

---

## ⏱️ **Data retention**

Workato applies data retention policies at the account level. You get **control over how long data is stored**.

📌 **Retention policy summary:**

| Plan  Maximum retention    | Customizable? |                            |
| -------------------------- | ------------- | -------------------------- |
| **None (default)**         | **30 days**   | ❌ No                       |
| **Enterprise Workspace**   | **90 days**   | ✅ Yes (**1-hour minimum**) |
| **Data Monitoring add-on** | **90 days**   | ✅ Yes (1-hour minimum)     |

 
> The Advanced Security and Compliance (ASC) add-on delivers the 90-day customizable retention.

---

### ⚙️ Customizing the data retention period

Customer administrators or account owners can navigate to:

> **Account Settings → Data retention**

There you can:

- **📏 Select the retention period unit** — Hours or Days.
- **🔢 Specify the number** of hours or days depending on the unit selected.

---

### ❓ Data retention FAQ

**What if we configure a retention value greater than the max?** It will be reset to the maximum retention policy of **30 or 90 days**, depending on the plan. Values less than the maximum are honored as-is.

**When does data get deleted?** Data older than the applicable **30 or 90 days** is deleted permanently.

**What types of data get deleted?**

> 📌 Four data types are deleted per the retention policy:
> 
> - **📨 Trigger event data**
> - **📋 Job summary** (e.g. title)
> - **📊 Custom job report**
> - **⚙️ Input and output details of each step in the recipe**

**How can we store data longer than 30/90 days?** Use **going-forward job history streaming** to store data in your event management app of choice. Workato supports **S3, Sumo Logic, Splunk, Datadog**, and any HTTP endpoint.

**What's included in the Advanced Security & Compliance (ASC) add-on?** Max 90-day retention with **customizable value down to 1 hour**. Contact your CSM to learn more.

**What happens if ASC is turned off?** Account resets to **30-day max retention** with no customization. All older-than-30-days data is deleted permanently.

## 📡 **Monitoring and auditing — the recommended pattern**

> 📌 **Recommended best practice**: use a **centralized log aggregation solution** and stream two things from Workato:
> 
> - **📜 "Job History"**
> - **👤 "Login & User Activity"**

Streaming logs gives your organization:

- **🎯 Control over your logs** at the SIEM/observability layer.
- **🔍 A holistic view of integration process flow** across multiple systems.
- **⚡ Rapid troubleshooting** — no need to log into multiple tools.

Compatible destinations: **S3, Sumo Logic, Splunk, Datadog**, or any HTTP-endpoint-based system.
